ABOUT US

Discover what makes NYP NCC (Air) unique. Our program offers military training of drills, Precision Drills (PDS) and Aerospace and so much more combined with leadership development.

Learn about our mission, and the values that guide our cadets to excellence in this CCA.

THE AIR JOURNEY

Experience the thrilling journey of our cadets from beginner to advanced aerospace missions, where they learn to fly remote-controlled drones and even model aircraft. It's hands-on, high-flying action that takes your skills to the next level!

Our cadets get to master Precision Drill Squad (PDS), where they perform sharp, high-impact rifle drills using replica rifles showcasing thrilling routines and impressive coordination in front of a live audience. It's discipline, teamwork, and showmanship all in one!

OUR EVENTS

Participate in our exciting lineup of events throughout the year, from air shows to leadership camps and even overseas trips!

These activities provide practical experiences that complement classroom learning and build camaraderie with others from other schools!
